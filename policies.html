Privacy & Refund Policy

1. Refund & Cancellation Policy

We believe in our methods, but we also respect Indian Consumer Protection laws. Here are our refund terms:

  • 3-Day Cooling-Off Period: You may cancel your subscription for a full refund within 3 days of the original purchase date if you are unsatisfied with the initial consultation.
  • How to Claim: To request a refund, email us at info@sk-elitefitness.in within 72 hours of payment.
  • After 3 Days: Once the 3-day window has passed, all training packages and diet plans become 100% non-refundable.
  • Session Cancellations (1-on-1): You must provide 24 hours' notice to cancel a scheduled live session. Less than 24 hours' notice will result in the session being forfeited.
  • Medical Freeze: Memberships may be paused for medical reasons (with a valid doctor's note) for up to 30 days. This extends your validity but does not grant a cash refund.

2. Privacy Policy (Data Protection)

We respect your privacy and comply with standard data protection norms.

  • What We Collect: We collect Name, Phone, Email, and Health Metrics (Weight, Height, Injuries) to create your plan.
  • Data Usage: Your data is used strictly for coaching purposes. We do not sell or trade your data to third parties.
  • Photo Privacy: We will never post your transformation photos on social media (Instagram/Facebook) without your explicit written or verbal permission.
  • Security: We use secure servers and do not store sensitive payment information (like Card Numbers or UPI PINs). All payments are handled by Razorpay.

3. Cookie Policy

Our website uses standard cookies to improve your browsing experience and remember your login details. You can disable cookies in your browser settings if you prefer.
